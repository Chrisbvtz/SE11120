Characters

Looney Tunes is a series of animated short films produced by Warner Bros. starting in 1930. The franchise became famous for its iconic characters such as Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, and Porky Pig, and is known for its trademark phrase, “That’s all, folks!” The series was a staple of Saturday morning cartoons and has evolved into a broader media franchise that includes television shows, films, and merchandise.
