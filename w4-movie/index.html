Spirited Away is a coming-of-age fantasy film directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It follows a young girl named Chihiro who becomes trapped in a mysterious and magical world filled with spirits, gods, and creatures after her parents are transformed into pigs. To save her family, she must work in a bathhouse run by a witch and learn the value of courage, love, and identity.

Company Credits: Studio Ghibli, Toho

Release Date: July 20, 2001

Genres: Animation, Fantasy, Adventure

Rating: PG

Running Time: 125 minutes
